About Me

With a background in leadership and process management, I am transitioning into the field of cybersecurity, bringing with me a wealth of experience in strategic operations, risk management, and team coordination. Over the past few years, I’ve progressed through roles at Cleveland-Cliffs and the United States Air Force, where I learned how to drive operational efficiency, foster innovation, and lead teams through complex challenges. These experiences have helped me develop valuable soft skills in leadership, problem-solving, and communication—skills that I’m now applying to the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity. Though still early in my cybersecurity career, I’m driven by a passion for protecting critical infrastructure and mitigating cyber risks. I hold active certifications, including CompTIA Security+, AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, and GPEN (SANS 2024), which provide a solid technical foundation as I continue to grow my skills. Additionally, I am working on obtaining the following certifications: SANS FOR508 (GCFA), SEC542 (GWAPT), and SEC660 (GXPN). I’m deeply committed to continuous learning and improvement, always seeking out new opportunities to expand my knowledge and refine my skills. My training as a Cyber Effects Officer in the Air Force has further sharpened my ability to navigate strategic challenges, and I’m eager to apply this mindset to secure digital systems and contribute to organizational success.
